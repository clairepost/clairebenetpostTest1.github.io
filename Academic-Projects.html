Endangered Language Project

Over the summer of 2021, I worked with Professor Khalil Iskaskourous through a SURF grant to create more language resources for endangered language learners. Guarni Web Corpus Site

In conjunction with Professor Maria Luisa Zubizarreta and Abigail Jarvis, I helped to create a GuarniWeb corpus site. The website contains 15 glossed Guaraní stories in addition to morpheme resources. The website will soon be publically available.
